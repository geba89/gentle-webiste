Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Gentle is designed with your privacy as a priority. We don't collect, track, or sell your data. Period.

Data Storage

All your habit data is stored locally on your device. We do not have servers that receive or store your information. Your habits, check-ins, notes, and achievements never leave your phone unless you explicitly choose to share them.

iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync, your data is stored in your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit technology. This means:

  • Your data is encrypted and protected by Apple
  • Only you can access it through your Apple ID
  • We cannot see, access, or read your synced data
  • Your data syncs only to your other Apple devices

Photos Access

Gentle requests photo library access only when you choose to save a monthly report image. We:

  • Only write images you explicitly save
  • Never read or access your existing photos
  • Don't upload photos anywhere

Notifications

Daily reminder notifications are scheduled locally on your device. We do not use push notification servers. All notification preferences are stored on your device only.

Analytics & Tracking

We do not use any analytics, tracking, or crash reporting tools. We have no idea who you are, how often you use the app, or what habits you're building. That's intentional.

Third-Party Services

Gentle does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or data brokers. There are no hidden trackers involved.

Subscriptions & Purchases

Gentle offers an optional "Gentle Pro" subscription. Here's how purchase data is handled:

  • Payment Processing: All payments are processed by Apple through the App Store. We never see, store, or have access to your payment information (credit card, bank details, etc.)
  • Subscription Status: We use Apple's StoreKit framework to verify your subscription status locally on your device. This data stays on your device
  • No Account Required: You don't need to create an account with us to subscribe. Your Apple ID handles everything
  • Apple's Privacy: Apple may collect data related to your purchase as described in their Privacy Policy

Data Sharing

We don't share your data with anyone because we don't have your data. The only way your information leaves your device is if you:

  • Share a monthly report via the share sheet
  • Enable iCloud sync (to your own account)

Children's Privacy

Gentle does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app contains no account creation, social features, or data collection mechanisms.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this privacy policy, we will update this page with the new date. Given our commitment to not collecting data, significant changes are unlikely.
